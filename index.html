About

Hi, I'm Ana! I'm a UC Berkeley Electrical Engineering and Computer Science M.S. graduate specializing in AI applications, with a strong focus on improving cutting-edge ML methodology to address complex social and industrial challenges.

With two years and counting as a Technology R&D Specialist at Accenture, I've designed and implemented innovative and practical M.L. and Knowledge Graphs methods, bringing LLM data analytics and semantic insights to both internal and external projects. Prior to this, my Master's thesis focused on employing Deep Reinforcement Learning to form large-scale classroom study groups, deepening my expertise in custom M.L. models and social networking/demographic analytics. I'm driven by the potential of emerging technologies to create meaningful change and provide insights into social dynamics.

Research Interests

  • Computing for social science and education
  • Bias and ethics in ML model deployment
  • Semantic data modeling
  • Recommendation systems
  • Online social network evolution, and information dispersal
